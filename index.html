About the Villa!

A beautiful 1+1 BHK villa in Morjim, Goa located on the banks of the Chapora river with picturesque views of the Goan coastline and the Chapora fort across the river. The villa is a short 5 minute drive from Morjim Beach, and in close proximity to the villages of Siolim, Ashwem and Arambol. The house comes fully furnished, with a large private garden that overlooks the river and a caretaker on call 24 x 7. It is most suited for couples and families.
